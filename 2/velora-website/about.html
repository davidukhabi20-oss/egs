Our Legacy in the Making

A journey of transforming the agricultural landscape of Nigeria through purpose, innovation, and operational excellence.

The Story

From Seed to Scale

Velora was born out of a critical observation: the immense tragedy of post-harvest loss in the tomato value chain. Watching thousands of tons of produce rot in fields while the country imported processed paste was a call to action.

Our founder envisioned a company that wouldn't just "sell products," but would solve a systemic failure. By building an industrial-grade processing bridge between the rural farm and the urban kitchen, Velora is turning vulnerability into value.
