Who We Are

Solving the Post-Harvest Crisis

Nigeria loses nearly 40% of its tomato harvest due to poor storage and processing infrastructure. Velora exists to bridge this gap.

We don't just make paste; we build resilient value chains. By industrializing the processing of locally grown tomatoes, we ensure farmers get fair value, consumers get premium products, and Nigeria moves toward food sovereignty.
