Investment Thesis

The Opportunity

The Nigerian tomato market is characterized by extreme volatility and inefficiency. While demand is constant, the lack of industrial processing creates a multi-billion dollar gap in the domestic value chain.

Velora is positioned not merely as a manufacturer, but as a critical infrastructure provider. By integrating sourcing, processing, and distribution, we mitigate risk and capture value at every stage of the chain.
